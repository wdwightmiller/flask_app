Guidelines
  • Rotation blocks should typically be 7 days (1 week)
  • Start date should typically be a Monday
  • End date should typically be a Sunday
  • Each block should have exactly one Friday
  • Evaluations will be sent on that Friday
Example:

Name: ICU Week 1 - Jan 2025
Start: Monday, Jan 6, 2025
End: Sunday, Jan 12, 2025
Friday: Jan 10, 2025
