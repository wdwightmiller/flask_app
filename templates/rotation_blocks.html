About Rotation Blocks

Rotation blocks represent weekly periods during which fellows are assigned specific evaluations. Each block:

  • Should typically be 7 days (1 week, Monday-Sunday)
  • Contains one Friday when evaluations are sent
  • Can have multiple fellows and multiple surveys assigned
  • Evaluations are sent only on the Friday during that specific week
  • Tip: Create blocks for multiple weeks at once to plan ahead efficiently
